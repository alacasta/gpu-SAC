Gpu-SAC

GPU implementation of the SAC-SMA model

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

GPU-SAC is a GPU implementation of the Sacramento Soil Moisture Accounting (SAC-SMA) model. This hydrological model takes into account soil moisture content and forecast precipitation as well as temperatures and it simulates runoff. More information can be found here and here. The implementation is fully oriented to nVidia GPUs using the nVidia's CUDA SDK.

About

Initial development of GPU-SAC is product of a research stay of A. Lacasta under the supervision of P. Restrepo at North Central River Forecast Center at NOAA. This stay was funded by Spanish Ministry of Economy and Competitiveness under the grant EEBB-I-15-10317
